The Dental Board of Australia ▼

Registers dental practitioners and students, develops professional standards and guidelines, and handles notifications and complaints.

The Australian Health Practitioner Regulation Agency (AHPRA) ▼

Responsible for the National Registration and Accreditation Scheme for over 530,000 health practitioners in Australia, including dentists, oral health therapists, and dental hygienists.

The Australian Dental Association Inc. (ADA) ▼

A voluntary membership association for dentists that promotes oral and general health, and supports its members in providing quality care.

The Dental Assistants Professional Association Inc. (DAPA) ▼

The peak national body representing dental assistants. It advocates for mandatory training and registration, promotes dental assisting as a career, and offers nationally recognized training courses.

The Dental Hygienists Association of Australia (DHAA) ▼

Represents dental hygienists, therapists, and oral health therapists, and supports the development of these professions.

Australian Dental Prosthetists Association (ADPA) ▼

The peak body for dental prosthetists and their patients.
